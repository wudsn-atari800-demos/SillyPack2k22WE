nyd23_invite by Buddy & PPs
---------------------------
Music demo for all A8 with at least 48kB of memory. Hope you enjoy listening
and get motivated to produce something for next New Years Disc :)
Greetings to all the people who can attend to sv22we!

- music and logo done by Buddy
- font and putting all together by PPs
- NTSC & PAL friendly
- automatic stereo POKEY replay
- songs replayed with LZSS routines based on VinsCool and DMSC work - stereo
  detection and playback by PPs
- logo created with g2f by TeBe
- xex crunched from 35925 bytes using SuperPacker by TeBe
